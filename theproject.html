The Project

In this study, 14 students took part in a coaching programme, discussing anything from careers to confidence. I interviewed them several times about how they thought they were growing and got some amazing insights about growth at university and growth due to coaching, which you can use in your life to help you grow.

About the Project

The context

This research comes at a time of concern of student mental health. Suicide rates in UK universities have never been higher and there has been a fivefold increase in student disclosure of mental health problems in a decade (BBC).

Find out more

Questions that research up to this point has not answered:

Is growth just a mindset or a perception that can be cultivated, a perception rather than an actuality?
Does it make sense to separate normative from non-normative growth?

The impact

My research is about an aspect of the human condition, namely growth, and how this is experienced by undergraduates in the context of coaching. I argue that growth can be intentionally stimulated through coaching
